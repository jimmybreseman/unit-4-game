Crystal Game

How to play -


  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below.

  • Blue
  • Green
  • Red
  • Yellow

  • When clicking on a crystal you will add a points to your total score.
  • You win the game when total score equals random number.
  • You lose the game when total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts the game will change the values of each crystal.
